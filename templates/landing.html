About BattyCoda

BattyCoda was developed to assist researchers in analyzing bat vocalizations and identifying species based on their calls. The platform combines advanced signal processing, machine learning, and a user-friendly interface to streamline the analysis workflow.

Our tools help save research time while providing accurate results for ecological surveys, monitoring programs, and conservation efforts.

Key Features

  • Upload and manage audio recordings
  • Automatic and manual segmentation of bat calls
  • Spectrogram visualization with adjustable parameters
  • Species identification through machine learning
  • Collaborative workspaces for research teams
  • Data organization through projects and task batches
  • Customizable theming with Maisonnette design system
